For more than 20 years I have been working with AI. My passion is helping organizations turn modern AI — and especially agentic AI — into shipped products, working processes and decisions that matter.

The arc: software-agent research at VTT (2001+) and Aalto/TKK, retail forecasting at unicorn-scale RELEX (CTO), the HUS Data Lake cloud-AI platform at TietoEVRY, AI in international tax at Aibidia (early LLM & RAG), and the NLP→LLM platform renewal at TalentAdore. Day to day I am a heavy user of frontier LLMs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google) and the leading agentic coding tools — Claude Code, OpenAI Codex, GitHub Copilot Pro and Cursor. Two working principles guide the practice:

  • Demand the impossible from agents. Push them well past obvious tasks, into the work that previously needed a senior engineer.

In my PhD at Aalto I dig into the internals of modern LLM architectures — to understand exactly where Transformer-based AI starts to bend, so that benchmarks, agent designs and product decisions match the real model limits rather than the marketing.

Behind the AI work sits 15+ years of senior technical leadership — 4× CTO across four sectors (retail unicorn RELEX, healthcare Hifian as founder, tax Aibidia, HR TalentAdore), scaling teams, services and SLAs through the toughest international growth phases.
